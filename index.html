新黃竹坑圖卷
New Scheme of Wong Chuk Hang

杜氏以傳統工筆畫技術細緻記錄黃竹坑都市的日夜變遷軌迹；並融合AR科技，以動態影像與觀眾互動連接，構築一個多彩多樣的當代工筆城市圖景。

Tao meticulously records the trajectory of the day-and-night changes in the urban area of Wong Chuk Hang using traditional Gongbi painting techniques; he also integrates AR technology, establishing interactive connections with the audience through dynamic images to construct a colorful and diverse contemporary Gongbi urban landscape.

作品簡介
Artwork Introduction

藝術家構想在公共空間中構建一處私人領域，於巴士站屏幕之內繪製城市屏幕，以「屏中屏」的概念，讓公眾參與並共同建構全新的觀看體驗。觀眾可在 AR 介面裡的工筆城市中，點選心儀的屏幕並上傳個人相片；相片將透過濾鏡轉化為工筆畫風格，即時呈現在對應屏幕之上。所有相片及項目相關素材將自動儲存至雲端系統，並有機會於項目第二階段，於四月九至十三日於香港視覺藝術中心展覽廳展出。

The artist envisions constructing a private realm within public space, painting urban screens inside bus shelter. Through the concept of “screen within a screen”, the public is invited to participate and co‑create a new viewing experience. Within the AR interface of the Gongbi city, visitors may select their preferred screen and upload personal photographs. The images will be transformed into a Gongbi painting style via a filter and displayed in real time on the corresponding screen. All photos and project-related materials will be automatically stored in the cloud system and may be exhibited at the Exhibition Hall of the Hong Kong Visual Arts Centre from 9 to 13 April during the second phase of the project.
